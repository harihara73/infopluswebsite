Pharmaceutical Engineering

The Plate and frame press filter which is the simplest
form of pressure filter. Where a number pf plates and frames are used to
increase the surface area for filtration.
The Rotary drum filter is a filtration process
in which the Slurry is filtered through a sieve-like surface of a rotating drum,
under applied vacuum conditions. The mechanism involved in this is surface filtration.
Sieve shaker is a machine which is used for sifting
or separation of powders into various sizes. The principle of sieve shaker is
based on vibration, agitation or gyration
Sigma blade mixer is mainly used to form dough like substances
in both small scale and large-scale industries. It belongs to
the family called double arm kneader mixer
Fluid bed dryers are used to reduce the moisture content
from wet granules. It works on the principle of fluidization of loaded substances.
In this process hot air passes through moist granules on a perforated bed,
where the granules are lifted up and suspended in the airstream and the
moisture is removed from the particles.
